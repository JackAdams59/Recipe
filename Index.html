Apple Strudel

This apple strudel recipe is very easy to make with apples, raisins, and frozen puff pastry, yet it looks impressive and tastes amazing! A truly delicious fall dessert.



Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Brown Sugar
  • Golden Raisens
  • Flour
  • Puff Pastry
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Milk
    •

    Instructions

    1. Stir the apples, brown sugar, golden raisins, and flour in a bowl.
    2. Place each puff pastry on a lightly floured work surface and roll into two rectangles.
    3. Fill and seal each pastry, then transfer to a baking sheet.
    4. Brush on the egg wash and cut slits into the top.
    5. Bake until golden brown.
    6. Enjoy!
